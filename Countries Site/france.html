France

About

France is located on the western edge of Europe and borders Belgium, Luxembourg, Germany, Switzerland and Italy. France was founded in the year 1792 where before the territory was ruled by the Roman Empire and others. France is known for its tourist attractions, most of which are located in the city of Paris. Some other things that the country is known for is food such as the baguette, the croissant, cheese, and macaroons as well as wine and champagne. A popular place in France is The Palace of Versailles which was built in the 1600’s and uses a lot of gold. A time in history that they are known for is the French Revolution which left a great impact on the development of France.

Locations

Paris

Paris is a city located in the North of France and is the capital of the country. The city is nicknamed the city of lights, the city of love, the fashion capital of the world, and the city of Art. Paris is known for having many museums and is home to the world’s biggest art museum called the Louvre (pictured below), which is home to the world’s most famous painting, the Mona Lisa. Paris is considered the most romantic city in the entire world because of the artistic vibe. The most iconic location in Paris that everyone knows about is the Eiffel Tower (pictured above) which takes control of the sky in the city of Paris and lights up at night. Another major attraction is the Cathedrale of Notre-Dame which is a medieval catholic cathedral that is stunning. Paris is also known for its shopping scene with high-end fashion brands like Dior, Chanel, and Louis Vuitton.
